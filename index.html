About Us

When we developed our first cloud-based 3D printer and gifted it to a SHG group, it was a must-watching facial expression among all the group members. Full of surprise, confusion, and dilemma from an operation point of view. But it was a radical idea of our marketing team. It was a joyful moment for Team Tronix when the SHG group members managed to operate the 3D printer effortlessly.

Our 3D printers and business model based on “how to work faster and smarter”. Our 3D printers are not only just a machine to print products, but it is also a money-making model.

We’re leading the 3D printing industry for one reason: we work with the best people and for the common people. Many thanks to our incredible team, who push the industry limits every day

Tronix is trusted to help educational institutions, lab-establishing companies, STEM training organizations, SHGs, NGOs, stationery shops, studios, craft bakery and unemployed individuals stay ahead of the competition.

A great idea is nothing without the tools to make it happen. That is the reason, we do not stop with the 3D printer itself. We also provide materials, different parts, and expert consultation. Team Tronix can even print parts for you on-demand.

Our mission drives us to make technological and innovative products that redirect to the future and facilitate people to enjoy the convenience of technology. Team Tronix is committed to making a really big difference in the technology and electronics world. It starts with the 3D printer. We are investing knowledge and manpower in the ICT industry also.
